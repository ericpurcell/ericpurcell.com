About

As an experienced professional in the I.T. field, with a wide and varied background, I have been offering my services to businesses, non-profit groups and individuals in the Greater Toronto Area for over a decade.

After gaining my diploma in E-business from Durham College in Oshawa I relocated to Toronto and started working for an established online networking website. There I was able to gain much of my experience and training from hands-on work as the market and technology evolved.

I pride myself in my critical thinking, analysis and problem solving skills which allow me to bring a great level of efficiency and care to my clients no matter the job, big or small.

When I'm not working in the world of I.T. I'm in the studio recording up and coming talent, performing around town with my band the Harbour Sharks or going to one of my wife's art exhibitions. I love meeting new people and expanding my network and hope to connect with you soon!
