Toronto based drummer for Harbour Sharks and Mirian Kay. Past-Drummer for Tru-Paz and Crown-A-Thornz.
Murdacell is my dubstep/hiphop production alter ego, fusing electronic and organic elements like live drums/percussion and synths/samplers.
Since then I've been part of a backing band for PHS Studios, and played/performed with Tru-Paz, 20/20 Project, Protoje & Jah 9, Choclair, and Crown-A-Thornz.
In the summer of 2008 Dubstructor and I released an EP as Segfault with our friend Mag-T on vocals.
I spend the majority of my time working with the Harbour Sharks on our recordings and doing as many shows as possible.
In late summer 2012 I co-founded a Toronto based music review site called Hogtown Racket.
